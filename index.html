Process

How We Work

Our simple 4-step process ensures your furniture gets the best possible treatment.

1

Assessment

We inspect your furniture to determine the best cleaning approach based on fabric type and condition.

2

Pre-Treatment

Specialized solutions are applied to break down stains and prepare the fabric for deep cleaning.

3

Deep Cleaning

Using professional equipment, we extract dirt, allergens, and cleaning solutions from deep within the fibers.

4

Drying & Finishing

We accelerate drying and apply fabric protectors to help repel future stains.

About DirtBusters

Founded in 2015, DirtBusters was born from a simple idea: furniture deserves the same level of cleaning care as the rest of your home. Our team of certified cleaning specialists combines scientific cleaning methods with old-fashioned attention to detail.

We're not just cleaners - we're fabric care experts who understand how different materials respond to various cleaning agents and techniques. Our eco-friendly approach means we use solutions that are tough on dirt but gentle on your furniture and the environment.
